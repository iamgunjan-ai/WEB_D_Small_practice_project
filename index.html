Prime minster of India (2014-present)

Narendra Modi, born on September 17, 1950, is the 14th Prime Minister of India, serving since May 2014. A prominent leader of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP), Modi has been a key figure in Indian politics. Known for his charismatic oratory and leadership skills, he was the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014, overseeing significant economic development in the state. As Prime Minister, Modi has implemented various initiatives, including Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Clean India), demonetization, and the Goods and Services Tax (GST). His policies have garnered both support and criticism, making him a polarizing figure in Indian politics.
